Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never disclose your OTP, passwords, or bank information to anyone  
2. Always verify the URL of the website before giving sensitive information  
3. Never click on suspicious links received through emails or messages  
4. Use strong passwords for different accounts  
5. Enable 2FA for your accounts  
6. Only install apps from the Play Store or App Store  
7. Be cautious while dealing with messages that create a sense of urgency, such as “your account will be blocked”  
8. Keep your device and apps updated regularly  
9. Keep an eye on your bank transactions regularly  
10. Never use public Wi-Fi for transactions  

In case of cybercrime:
Report at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline Number: 1930
