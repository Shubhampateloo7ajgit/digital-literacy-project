DO’s (Responsible Use):

Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible
Think before you post — ensure your content is appropriate and respectful
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
Maintain privacy settings to control who can see your content
Use professional language when interacting in public or academic spaces
Update apps regularly to ensure security and performance
Build a positive digital footprint by sharing achievements and learning


DON’Ts (Avoid These Mistakes):

Do not share personal or sensitive information (address, OTP, passwords)
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Do not spread fake news or unverified content
Do not engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior
Do not overshare personal life details publicly
Do not use weak or common passwords
Do not accept friend requests from unknown people
Do not ignore privacy and security settings
